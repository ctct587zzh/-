本长亭动漫社由坠怜[甄子豪]在2026.1.10创立，汇聚众多二次元优秀爱好者，大家心怀热忱、携手共进，一同打造长亭专属氛围，共创组织盛世，本组织隶属于情海旗下，同时正式加盟神风和华兴会，同心同行，稳步发展。

This Changting Anime Club was founded by Zhui Lian (Zhen Zihao) on January 10, 2026. It brings together many outstanding lovers of anime and two-dimensional culture. Everyone works together with enthusiasm to create a unique atmosphere for Changting and build a prosperous community. The club is affiliated with Qinghai, and has officially joined Shenfeng and Huaxinghui. We forge ahead side by side and develop steadily.
